Our Mission

Kathmandu Institute aims to be a hub for research, education, and discourse on contemporary cities, with a focus on designing future urban landscapes.

The organisation will delve into the profound changes reshaping cities, driven by infrastructure projects, socio-economic shifts, and technological advancements, engaging experts from diverse fields in collective efforts toward city building.

About Us

Cities represent the pinnacle of civilization's achievements, each developing its unique form and culture over centuries, fostering economic competitiveness and innovation. However, rapid urbanization driven by liberal economics and media has led to haphazard development and environmental challenges.

Kathmandu Institute aims to tackle these issues by serving as a hub for interdisciplinary research, collaboration, and innovation, drawing on local expertise and international partnerships to pave the way for sustainable urban development.
